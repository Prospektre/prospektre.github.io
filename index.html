Prospektre

Prospekt (RU):

A very wide and long straight street, which serves as an arterial road.


Prospectus (Latin):

A view, or outlook.


Prospekte (DE):

A pamphlet or brochure, typically those handed out by grocery stores.


Prospector (EN):

A person who excavates in search of rare minerals.


Spectre (EN):

A ghost, or the idea of an unpleasant event that might happen in the future.
